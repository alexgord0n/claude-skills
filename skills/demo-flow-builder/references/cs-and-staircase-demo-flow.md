# CS Platform Demo Flow Reference

## Core Value Proposition
Gainsight CS centralizes customer data from every source, helps CSMs manage their book of business proactively, drives health scoring and risk signals, and scales CS operations -- from 1:1 high-touch to digital at scale.

---

## Demo Sections (in suggested order)

### 1. Exec View (CCO / VP CS)
**Value:** Board-ready visibility without manual reporting
- Health by region, product, segment
- Current NRR + GRR
- NPS of org
- Sharable (Gainsight Everywhere license)
- Fully configurable + lots OOTB

**Core value hook:** "What you report to the board -- all in one view, without asking your CSMs to pull it."

---

### 2. Manager View
**Value:** Portfolio health + team performance in one place
- Team health distribution
- CSM activity and performance
- Drill into individual CSM portfolios
- Operational reporting: tasks, time-to-close, activity types
- Drive 1:1s directly from the data

**Core value hook:** "Stop running 1:1s from spreadsheets. Run them from data."

---

### 3. CSM Day in the Life -- Gainsight Home
**Value:** One view to start the day -- everything prioritized
- Full BoB overview: companies, ARR, renewals, health distribution, NPS/CSAT/sentiment
- Configurable by role or user preference
- Cockpit: day-to-day workspace
  - CTAs: data- and lifecycle-driven alerts
  - Playbooks: standard processes per CTA type (NPS promoter = upsell outreach, 120 days to renewal = renewal playbook, low adoption = risk CTA)
  - Templatized tokenized emails -- sent from GS, auto-logged in Timeline
- Timeline: central location for all customer interactions
  - Note templates, email auto-log, @mention collaboration

**Core value hook:** "Every signal that matters, surfaced and actionable -- before it becomes a problem."

---

### 4. C360
**Value:** True single source of truth for any customer
**Use Case Frame:** Prepping for EBR with low adoption customer
- Journey milestone timeline (chronological)
- Attributes (industry, lifecycle stage, CSM, etc.)
- Health score trends, open CTAs, success plans, community engagement
- Configurable by what matters to each company

**Adoption (Usage Reports)**
- Product telemetry from PX, surfaced directly in CS view
- Feature adoption, logins, cohort benchmarking

**Scorecard**
- DEAR framework: Deployment, Engagement, Adoption, ROI
- Objective + subjective inputs
- Weighted and customizable
- NPS, support ticket volume, onboarding experience, CSM sentiment
- Community activity as a health signal
- Scorecard Optimizer: AI-powered recommendations to improve predictiveness

**Business Modeler**
- Model complex customer relationships: multiple products, deployments, BUs
- Health at every level without losing the overall picture
- Best in class for enterprise complexity

**Success Plans / Goals**
- Capture and track customer outcomes
- Joint goals with customer stakeholders
- Export to PowerPoint for EBR/QBR -- auto-pulls Gainsight reports
- Saves CSM prep time, demonstrates accountability

**Timeline (C360 level)**
- All interactions for a single customer
- Salesforce bidirectional sync
- Drives health score for engagement

---

### 5. Journey Orchestrator
**Value:** Scale CS without scaling headcount -- digital + human touch blended
- Admin-built automation for digital CS motions
- Embed reports, tokenized emails that look like they're from the CSM
- Logic built on customer behaviors and signals
- Pool model: route CTAs to available CSMs
- Use cases: low usage, onboarding, renewal, expansion
- Blends: emails + in-app engagements + education courses + community + CTAs

**Core value hook:** "Scale the processes that don't need a human touch. Bring in your CSMs when it actually matters."

---

## CS Stakeholder Map
| Stakeholder | What They Care About | Where to Focus |
|-------------|---------------------|----------------|
| CCO / VP CS | NRR, GRR, health visibility, board reporting | Exec View, Health Scores |
| CS Manager | Team performance, 1:1s, portfolio risk | Manager View, Cockpit |
| CSM | Daily efficiency, account prep, playbooks | GS Home, Cockpit, C360, Timeline |
| RevOps | Forecast, renewal pipeline, automation | Scorecards, JO, Success Plans |
| IT | Data integrations, Salesforce sync | Integrations, C360 data sources |

# Staircase AI Demo Flow Reference

## Core Value Proposition
Staircase AI surfaces real-time churn risk and growth signals by listening to all customer communication channels automatically -- no manual data entry. CSMs and execs get always-on intelligence without overhead.

## Setup / Framing
"Staircase offers rapid time to value -- uniquely real-time. It makes sure you're not missing any key moment in customer communications. Setup takes hours."

---

## Demo Sections

### 1. Exec Dashboard
**Value:** Holistic view of which accounts need attention -- without asking anyone
- View specific segment or full BoB
- Auto-generated health scores from listening signals
  - Multiple dimensions, no setup required
  - Yellow = benchmark, Green = exceeds, Red = needs attention
- Churn risk: proactive rather than reactive -- signals surface before customer is half out the door

---

### 2. Tree Map
**Value:** Instantly prioritize where to focus attention across the full portfolio
- Visual representation of account risk vs. size
- Helps managers and CSMs triage efficiently

---

### 3. Account Deep Dive (Abbett example)
**Value:** Get up to speed on any account in minutes -- without asking the CSM
- Notifications: key changes surfaced (leadership change, communication drop-off)
- Relationship Heatmap: who's engaged on both sides, where are you not multi-threaded?
- Radar: 360 visibility across listening signals

---

### 4. Team Efficiency Report
**Value:** Are you spending time on the right accounts?
- Communication volume vs. account health
- Are high-risk accounts getting enough attention?
- Where is time being wasted?

---

| CS Manager | Team prioritization, account visibility | Tree Map, Account Deep Dive |
