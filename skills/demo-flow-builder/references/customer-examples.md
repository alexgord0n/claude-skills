## HOW TO USE THIS FILE

When building a demo flow:
1. Match the prospect's **use case** to the relevant category above
2. Pick the example with the closest **industry or company profile** match
3. When presenting: explain *why this company is similar to them* -- "Klaviyo is similar to you in that they also needed search to span their help center and their academy..."
4. If a prospect has an active competitor relationship (e.g., migrating from Khoros), note Khoros-to-GS migrations: Gong, Airtable, Zoom, Adobe, Netskope, Nintex all moved off Khoros
