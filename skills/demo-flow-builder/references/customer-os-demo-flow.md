# Customer OS Demo Flow Reference

## Core Value Proposition
Gainsight's Customer OS shows how PX, Community (CC), Education (CE/Skilljar), CS Platform, and Staircase AI work together across the full customer journey -- from first login to renewal and expansion. The whole is greater than the sum of the parts.

## When to Use This Flow
- Executive or stakeholder overview calls (< 30 min, multi-product)
- New logo deals where the prospect hasn't seen any Gainsight product
- Cross-sell / expansion calls where the full platform story is relevant
- Conference demos or overview sessions

---

## < 5 Minute Version (Boardroom / Lightning Demo)

**Framing:**
"Today we're going to walk through how Gainsight helps your customers by guiding and educating them as they traverse your application, nurturing them through community, and continuing their education through digital classrooms -- ultimately creating happy, renewing, and growing customers. We'll see this from two perspectives: an end-user and a CSM."

**Arc:**

1. **Start in application (as new end-user of EmailMonkey / your product)**
   - Login → tracking starts immediately
   - Walk-through guide (images, videos, GIFs, tooltips)
   - NPS survey
   - KCB: self-serve portal
     - Recommended content, personalized onboarding checklist
     - Federated search: community + external KBs
     - "Here's an interesting article in the community..."

2. **Head to Customer Hub (Community)**
   - Q&A / peer answers
   - Knowledge Base (how-tos, technical docs)
   - Product feedback / ideas
   - Product news + updates
   - Groups and events
   - "I see I can do more formalized learning..."
   - CE courses, ILTs, learning paths → certifications (without leaving the hub)

3. **CS Backend (as CSM)**
   - GS Home: full BoB visibility from in-app telemetry + community activity + course progress + tickets
   - Cockpit CTAs: automatically generated across the customer journey → prescriptive playbooks
   - C360: health scores auto-driven by all data points; customer cheat sheet
   - Staircase AI: always-on view across emails, tickets, messages, calls, meetings, product usage
     - Example: extremely negative message → dig in quickly
     - Real-time alerts, stakeholder heatmap
   - Journey Orchestrator: digital + human touch at scale
     - Tokenized emails + in-app engagements + courses + community badging + CTAs + playbooks
     - Built-in logic and decision points

4. **Spaces (wrap)**
   - Secure collaboration space between customers and CSMs
   - Share account info, success plans, cases, usage, learner data
   - "Bringing it all together."

## Full Multi-Product Demo Flow (30-45 min)

Use the individual product reference files + sequence them as:

1. **PX** (end-user experience, analytics, engagements) -- 12-15 min
2. **CC** (community, federated search, ideas) -- 8-10 min
3. **CE/Skilljar** (learner experience, admin, analytics) -- 8-10 min
4. **CS Platform** (GS Home, C360, health scores, JO) -- 8-10 min
5. **Staircase AI** (exec dashboard, account view) -- 3-5 min
6. **Spaces / Wrap** -- 2 min

**Multi-product connective tissue:**
- PX telemetry → CS health scores → CTAs → JO emails
- CC activity → CS community engagement widget → health scores
- CE course completions → CS certifications → expansion signals
- KCB inside PX → pulls CC content + CE courses + KB articles
- Federated search: PX KCB + CC search → both surface Skilljar content
